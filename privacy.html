Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. iWellny is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. No servers store your data, no analytics track your behavior, no ads profile you.

Audio Processing

All audio processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's native frameworks. Sound mixing, volume adjustments, fade effects, and Memory Sounds recordings never leave your device.

Health Data

When you enable HealthKit, iWellny reads and writes metrics like mindful minutes and HRV. This data is handled exclusively through Apple's HealthKit and stored securely on your device. We never access it.

Location Services

Location data for Memory Sounds is processed locally to tag recordings. This data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to servers or third parties.

Microphone Access

Microphone is only used for Memory Sounds when you explicitly record. Recordings are stored locally. We never access your microphone without your action.

Notifications

Notifications are used solely for timer alerts (Sleep Timer, Pomodoro). Notification data is processed locally by iOS and is not stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

All settings, presets, and preferences are stored locally using iOS secure storage. If you use iCloud backup, data may be included in your encrypted backup managed by Apple.

Third-Party Services

iWellny does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No Firebase, Google Analytics, or Facebook SDK. The only external integration is Apple's App Store.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Contact us at support@iwellny.app
