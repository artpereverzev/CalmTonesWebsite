üìä Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. iWellny is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle, not an afterthought. We have no servers storing your data, no analytics tracking your behavior, and no advertising networks profiling you.

üéµ Audio Processing

All audio processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's native audio frameworks. This includes sound mixing, volume adjustments, fade effects, and Memory Sounds recordings. No audio data is ever transmitted to external servers.

‚ù§Ô∏è Health Data

When you enable HealthKit integration, iWellny can read and write certain health metrics like mindful minutes, heart rate variability, and sleep data. This data is handled exclusively through Apple's HealthKit framework and is stored securely on your device. We never have access to this information.

üìç Location Services

If you choose to enable location features for Memory Sounds, your location data is processed locally to tag recordings with place information. This location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

üé§ Microphone Access

Microphone access is only used for the Memory Sounds feature when you explicitly choose to record. Recordings are stored locally on your device. We never access your microphone without your explicit action, and recordings are never transmitted anywhere.

üîî Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are used solely for timer alerts (Sleep Timer completion, Pomodoro phase changes). Notification data is processed locally by iOS and is not stored or transmitted.

üíæ Data Storage

All app settings, sound presets, achievements, and user preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. If you use iCloud backup, this data may be included in your encrypted backup, which is managed entirely by Apple.

üö´ Third-Party Services

iWellny does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We don't use Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar services. The only external integration is Apple's App Store for purchases, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

üë∂ Children's Privacy

iWellny does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed to be safe and appropriate for users of all ages.

üìù Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

üìß Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or iWellny's privacy practices, please contact us at support@iwellny.app
