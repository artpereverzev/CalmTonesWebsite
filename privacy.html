Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. iWellny is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. No servers store your data, no analytics track your behavior, no ads profile you.

Audio Processing

All audio processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's native frameworks. Sound mixing, volume adjustments, fade effects, and Memory Sounds recordings never leave your device.

Health Data

When you enable HealthKit, iWellny reads and writes metrics like mindful minutes and HRV. This data is handled exclusively through Apple's HealthKit and stored securely on your device. We never access it.

Location Services

Location data for Memory Sounds is processed locally to tag recordings. This data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to servers or third parties.
