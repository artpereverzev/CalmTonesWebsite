Indie Solo Developer & Sound Enthusiast

I created iWellny as a personal journey to find peace in our increasingly chaotic digital world. As someone who struggled with focus and anxiety, I discovered the profound impact that carefully crafted ambient sounds can have on our mental state.

My mission is simple: to help you find your calm, whether you're trying to focus on deep work, meditate, or drift into peaceful sleep. Every sound in this app has been carefully selected and mixed to create the perfect atmosphere for relaxation and productivity.

"Built with love, passion, and countless hours of listening to rain sounds at 2 AM."
