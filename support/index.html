Frequently Asked Questions

How do I mix multiple sounds together?
Tap the "Mixer" tab to access our sound mixing studio. Add sounds with "+", adjust volumes with sliders, and save combinations as presets. Mix up to 70 sounds!
Can I use iWellny while using other apps?
Yes! iWellny supports background playback. Sounds continue when you switch apps, lock your device, or turn off the screen.
How do Live Activities work?
On iOS 16.1+, control iWellny from Lock Screen and Dynamic Island without opening the app. Enable in Settings → iWellny → Live Activities.
What are Memory Sounds?
Record ambient audio from meaningful places. Recordings are saved with location data so you can relive those moments anytime.
Is my data private?
Absolutely! All processing happens on your device. We don't collect or store personal information. See our Privacy Policy.

Troubleshooting

Audio Issues

  • Check device volume is turned up
  • Disable silent mode (mute switch)
  • Restart the app
  • Ensure sufficient storage space

Background Playback

  • Enable Background App Refresh in Settings
  • Disable Low Power Mode
  • Check background audio permissions

Live Activities

  • Requires iOS 16.1 or later
  • Check Face ID & Passcode settings
  • Enable in Settings → iWellny
